Most popular!! Spy X Family

Spy x Family" is a gripping manga series by Tatsuya Endo. The story follows Twilight, a spy on a mission to infiltrate an elite school. To maintain his cover, he forms an unconventional family by marrying powerful woman named Yor and adopting a telepathic girl named Anya. The series seamlessly combines espionage, action, and humor, creating a compelling narrative filled with unexpected twists. "Spy x Family" has gained widespread popularity for its intriguing characters and unique blend of genres, making it a must-watch for anime and manga enthusiasts.

Tearmoon Empire

"Tearmoon Empire" refers to a Japanese light novel series written by Nozomu Mochitsuki and illustrated by Giluna. The story falls within the genres of comedy, fantasy, and isekai (a subgenre where characters are transported to another world). "Tearmoon Empire" follows the comedic and fantastical adventures of Princess Mia, a young and naive ruler of the Tearmoon Empire. After being reincarnated into her favorite otome game, Mia must navigate the challenges of her new life while trying to avoid the tragic fate that awaits her character in the game. With her eccentric and sometimes comical decisions, Mia transforms the empire and its inhabitants, turning it into an unexpected force to be reckoned with.
